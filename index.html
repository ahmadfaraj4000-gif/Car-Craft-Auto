What We Do

Our Services

Two shops in one — expert collision restoration and full mechanical repairs, all under one roof.

Body Shop
Collision & Body Repair
Accident damage, bodywork & paint restoration
01
Collision Damage Repair
Fix damage from car accidents. Repair bent frames and body panels. Replace damaged parts like doors, bumpers, and fenders.
02
Frame Straightening
Hydraulic precision frame straightening to restore your vehicle's structural integrity and alignment for safety.
03
Dent Removal
Paintless dent repair (PDR) and panel reshaping for hail damage and minor collision dents — no repainting needed.
04
Panel Replacement
Doors, hoods, trunks, quarter panels, and bumpers replaced with precision fit and finish matching factory standards.
05
Painting & Refinishing
Computer color-matching, primer, paint, and clear coat application for a seamless factory finish every time.
06
Surface Preparation
Professional sanding, grinding, body filler application, and masking to prep surfaces for a perfect paint job.
07
Glass Replacement
Windshield, side window, and rear glass replacement or repair using OEM-grade materials.
08
Rust Repair
Removal of rusted metal, panel replacement, and corrosion protection to extend your vehicle's life.
09
Insurance Estimates
We work directly with your insurance company, write detailed repair estimates, and document all accident damage.
Mechanic Shop
Mechanical Services
Diagnostics, maintenance & mechanical repairs
01
Auto Engine Diagnostic
Computer-based engine diagnostics to identify check engine lights, fault codes, and performance issues fast and accurately.
02
Brake Service & Repair
Full brake inspections, pad and rotor replacement, brake line repair, caliper service, and ABS diagnostics.
03
Oil Change
Conventional and full synthetic oil changes with a multi-point vehicle inspection included at no extra charge.
04
Tires
Tire mounting, balancing, rotation, and flat repair. We'll help you find the right tire for your vehicle and budget.
05
Battery
Battery load testing, replacement, and terminal cleaning for all makes and models — no appointment needed.
06
Air & Cabin Filter Replacement
Engine air filter and cabin air filter swap to keep your engine breathing clean and your interior air fresh.
07
Exhaust Repair
Muffler, catalytic converter, flex pipe, and exhaust manifold inspection and repair — we fix leaks, rattles, and failures.
08
Body & Trim
Interior and exterior trim repair, molding replacement, and body panel refinishing for a clean, finished look.
09
Key Fob Replacement
Lost or broken key fob? We program and replace key fobs for most makes and models — fast, affordable, no dealership needed.
