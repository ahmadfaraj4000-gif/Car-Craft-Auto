Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Overview

This Cookie Policy explains how Car Craft Auto Body & Towing uses cookies and similar browser storage technologies on this website. It should be read with our Privacy Policy.

At this time, the homepage does not use advertising pixels or behavioral advertising cookies. The site uses necessary browser storage for functionality and preferences.

Technologies We Use

  • Local storage: used to remember whether you accepted or denied the cookie banner and whether the rental assistance promo was already shown.
  • Form and upload services: Convex and Formspree may process technical information needed to receive forms, upload photos, store files, and protect services from abuse.
  • Admin authentication: the private admin portal may use authentication/session cookies or storage necessary for staff login and security.
  • External links: if you click Google Maps, Facebook, or other third-party links, those sites may use their own cookies or tracking technologies.

Current Browser Storage Keys

  • carcraftCookieConsent: remembers whether you selected Accept or Deny on the homepage cookie banner.
  • carcraftRentmectPromoSeen: remembers whether the rental assistance promotional modal has been shown.

Your Choices

You can accept or deny non-essential browser storage using the cookie banner on the homepage. You can reopen preferences from the homepage footer. You can also clear cookies, local storage, and site data through your browser settings.

Changes

If we add analytics, advertising pixels, retargeting, or other non-essential tracking, we will update this Cookie Policy and the site’s cookie choices.
