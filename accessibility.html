Site Policy

Accessibility

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Our Commitment

Car Craft Auto Body & Towing is committed to making our website usable and accessible for all visitors, including people with disabilities. We work toward practical alignment with recognized accessibility guidance such as WCAG.

Accessibility Measures

  • Semantic page structure and descriptive page titles.
  • Alternative text for meaningful images where appropriate.
  • Keyboard-accessible links, buttons, and form controls.
  • Visible labels for form fields and clear error/success messaging.
  • Responsive layouts designed for mobile and desktop screens.
  • Color contrast and readable text sizing reviewed as part of site updates.

Known Limitations

Some third-party services, external pages, maps, social media pages, uploaded images, or embedded provider experiences may not be fully controlled by us. We continue to review and improve the public website as issues are found.

Need Help?

If you have trouble using any part of this website or need information in another format, please contact us. We will make reasonable efforts to help you access the information or service you need.
